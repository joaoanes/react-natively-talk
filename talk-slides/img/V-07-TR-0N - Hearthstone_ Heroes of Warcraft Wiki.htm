V-07-TR-0N
Set: Goblins vs Gnomes
Type: Minion
Subtype: Mech
Rarity: Legendary
Cost: 8
Attack: 4
Health: 8
Abilities: Charge, Mega-Windfury

Charge Mega-Windfury (Can attack four times a turn.)

See this card on Hearthpwn

[data page]

V-07-TR-0N is an uncollectible legendary neutral minion card, from the Goblins vs Gnomes set. It is summoned through Mimiron's Head's triggered effect.

For more information, see Mimiron's Head.

Lore[edit | edit source]

V-07-TR-0N is inspired by the composite mechanical boss V0-L7R-0N in Ulduar. This in turn is a reference to Voltron,[1] a cartoon series featuring a number of robots which could combine to form a huge "Super Robot". This inspiration was later referenced heavily with the introduction of the animation for Mimiron's Head's triggered effect.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • V-07-TR-0N is the only card to feature the Mega-Windfury ability, introduced with Goblins vs Gnomes.
